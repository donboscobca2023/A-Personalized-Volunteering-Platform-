About Our Platform

The proposed system is a comprehensive, web-based volunteering portal designed to connect individuals with social impact projects that align with their skills, interests, and availability. It streamlines volunteer engagement, enables data-driven coordination for NGOs, and fosters a more structured, impactful, and rewarding volunteering ecosystem.
